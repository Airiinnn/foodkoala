• About •

FoodKoala is a food pickup app. But it's not just any food pickup app. It is an excess food app.
FoodKoala aims to reduce food wastage at the retail and consumer levels by allowing retailers and consumers to sell them at lower prices.







• How to use •

Step 1: Select your preferred location to see all the available, delicious dishes in that area!
Step 2: Find one (or a few dishes) which are irresistible to your palate.
Step 3: Contact the seller via the mobile number listed to work out an ideal time for you to pick it up!

Ordering is that simple.
